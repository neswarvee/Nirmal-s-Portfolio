Project Overview

Developed an online store for Trains of Sheffield, allowing customers to browse model railway products, create orders, and manage banking details.

Project Details

  • Designed a user-friendly interface that enables customers to easily navigate product categories, view details, and add items to their order.
  • Implemented a robust authentication system for customer and staff registration, ensuring secure login and personal information management.
  • Created a staff area for inventory management, allowing staff to add, edit, and delete products while updating stock levels automatically.
  • Integrated an order management system that tracks order status (pending, confirmed, fulfilled) and allows customers to confirm their orders with banking details.
  • Established a manager role with exclusive permissions to promote or demote staff members, ensuring a secure user role hierarchy.
