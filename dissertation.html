Dissertation Project

Investigating memorization in recurrent neural networks (RNNs) to demonstrate that memorization is not capped due to numerical instability.

Project Details

  • Investigating memorization in recurrent neural networks (RNNs) to demonstrate that memorization is not capped due to numerical instability.
  • Developing a proof of concept that regenerates historical states using matrix inversion and invertible non-linearity.
  • Exploring methods to maintain stability while increasing sequence length and efficiently compressing information into limited states.
  • Putting the theory into simulation using Python to prove my hypotheses.
